"Hey now, you're useless. If you're in the 'best friend' position of a galge, you have to be an information expert who can smoothly answer what the protagonist asks."

Even a best friend character in a game wouldn't expect to be asked for information about a male classmate instead of the heroines.

The fact that I occasionally showed detailed knowledge was why I couldn't escape the galge best friend position, but sadly, that was just who Otto Suwen was.
Anyway, with Otto added to the festival party, our adventure continued.

"That's right, our festival starts here!"

"It feels like it's going to end here!"

This was indeed Otto Suwen.

"—As you all know, the Cherry Blossom Festival will be held today. I'm sure many of you will be going, but please do not get too carried away. Please make sure to act with the moderation expected of a student."

During afternoon homeroom, while everyone was in a restless mood because of the festival afterward, I was singled out for a warning by our very own Frederica-sensei.
Acting with the moderation expected of a student—there should be absolutely no reason for me to be warned.

It was true that I was the one who proposed gathering a party for the festival under the pretext of a "plan to increase Emilia's friends."

"But that's not a strange thing to say, right?"

"And yet, that's meaningful?"

"Feri-chan doesn't really care? But, but, since you invited Feri-chan, naturally you invited Crusch-sama too, right?"

"That's... ah, no way?"

I finally understood what Ferris was trying to say, and I looked over Ferris's head to Crusch, who was sitting on the other side.

"Do not worry. You said the more people, the better, did you not? So, I have called out to as many as possible. We should expect a fair number of personnel."

"I didn't want a labor force!"

Crusch gave a slightly off-target answer, but she was the Student Council President. Not only that, her family was one of the most prominent in the city, so she had connections everywhere.
From Crusch's misunderstanding, it seemed likely that the people she called out to weren't limited to those within the school.

"That's why it might turn into a much larger group than Subaru-kyun thinks, nya."

"Subaru-kyun is a dummy."

Frederica-sensei at the teacher's desk clapped her hands to stop the battle between us two small animals to decide who was at the top.

Crusch easily took the bait from Priscilla, who was playfully provoking those around her.

Because the fun days are still just beginning.
