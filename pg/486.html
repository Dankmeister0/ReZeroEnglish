Re:Zero EX: "School Re:Zero! 3rd Period! (Vainglory IF)"

※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※ ※

This story is an April Fools' project.

First, please read "School Re:Zero! 1st Period!" and "School Re:Zero! 2nd Period!" before proceeding.

This is Re:Zero transplanted into a modern school setting with a complete IF setup.

Although it is a modern setting, the characters' names, hair colors, and so on are accepted as they are without any sense of incongruity.

Since it follows many of the tropes of gal games, it might be a bit much for some.

However, no one becomes unhappy.

With the above in mind, if you have a sense of humor and can forgive the characters being happy, please proceed with that understanding.

<<School Re:Zero! 3rd Period!!>>

—My awakening every morning usually comes from a sudden impact.

"—Come on, wake up already, I suppose!"

"Honduras!?"

Accompanied by a light voice, an impact that could hardly be called light hit me directly in the stomach. I woke up with a scream, and right before my eyes was my lovely sister's face again today.

"...Morning, Beako. You're pretty, cutie, and lovely today too."

"Of course, that is exactly right, I suppose. Today, as every day, Betty is pretty, I suppose. Now, it's a pleasant morning, so wake up quickly and eat breakfast with me, I suppose."

Beatrice looked down at me with a smug smile while sitting on my stomach. With her light body still on top of me, I nodded, saying, "Alright, alright," and curled up right there.

"Ah! You don't understand at all, I suppose! Going back to sleep! That's the posture for going back to sleep, I suppose!"

"Ugh, understand me, Beako. I want to cherish the time I spend eating with you in this limited life. But, just as much, I want to enjoy this decadent morning time..."

"Muki—! I won't allow that, I suppose! Ni-cha... Subaru! Which is more important to you, Betty or going back to sleep, I suppose!"

"Asking something like a stubborn actress in a daytime soap opera... No, wait, that's an idea!"

"—? What did you suddenly think of... Ukyah!?"

I reached out to the little girl swaying on my stomach and dragged her into the futon. Stroking the head of my sister, who was now tucked snugly against my chest, I involved Beatrice in my warm, decadent second sleep. —This is killing two birds with one stone: my sister and a second sleep!

"With this, Beako can also enjoy a second sleep. Its effectiveness is double the happiness of a normal second sleep... no, it's no exaggeration to say it goes even higher as an exponent!"

"You've started saying stupid things again, I suppose! L-Let go, I suppose! Betty isn't a child anymore who needs to sleep with Subaru, I suppose!"

"Ehh, lately Beako hasn't been sleeping with me, and I'm so looooone-ly... So, I'll take this opportunity to fully ingest some Beatrizine. Sniff sniff."

"Don't sniff me, I suppose!"

The cute Beako struggled in my arms. By the way, "Beatrizine" is a special intracerebral substance I devised that can be ingested by touching or doting on Beatrice. If they don't ingest this, the men of the Natsuki family wither and die. The samples are myself and my father. And so, while playing with my sister like that, ironically, I ended up wide awake.

"Gugu, damn... Was this Beako's strategy? To use her own cute self as bait in a diversionary tactic to separate me from my sleepiness... I've been played..."

"I-I don't know what's what anymore, but just let go, I suppose. Ah, my hair and clothes that I worked so hard on are a mess now, I suppose."

"Don't worry. Even if you're a mess, your charm doesn't fade. There's a beauty in being a mess. Messy Beako."

"You're messy-loud, I suppose!!"

An angry Beatrice hopped off the bed and cutely stuck her tongue out at me. Scratching my head at my sister's tantrum, I stood up and tilted my head. "Huh?" The reason was the clock. Strictly speaking, the time the clock was pointing to. That was—

"Hey now, Beako. You woke me up an hour earlier than usual. You're being quite the scatterbrain. Come on, let's continue the second sleep."

"Don't spread your arms like that, I suppose! It makes me want to jump in, I suppose! That's not it, I woke you up an hour early this morning for a reason, I suppose. That's correct, I suppose."

"You say it's correct, but..."

"Starting this morning, Betty has a meeting for the school trip executive committee, I suppose. So, I'm going to eat breakfast earlier than usual and head to school feeling refreshed, I suppose."

"I see, and I'm being dragged into that... Why!?"

I protested against my drowsy time being interrupted by Beatrice's circumstances, which she stated while puffing out her chest. No matter how cute my sister is, there are things you can and can't do. I am firmly resolved to oppose such selfish ideas.

"My grudge for having my second sleep interrupted is deep. What logic can you use to defeat me? Come on, say it, Beako! Why did you wake up your big brother!"

"Because, if only Betty wakes up early, I won't get to say good morning or goodbye to Subaru, and I can't eat breakfast together, and I'll be lonely, I suppose..."

"Damn! So cute!"

In the end, a siscon had no power to reject his sister's cute wish! To respond to Beatrice's honest feelings, I gallantly got out of bed. It's true that I'm wide awake now, and it's too late to go back to sleep anyway. This is a time where responding to a cute sister's request is a brother's duty—.

"Alright, since it's decided, help me get dressed, Beako."

"Do it yourself, I suppose!"

As expected, she wouldn't go that far. Anyway, since I felt like there had been a mysterious period of stagnation, it was important to properly do the usual routine.

△▼△▼△▼△

"Well then, I'll be taking Beatrice-chan with me!"

"I'm being taken, I suppose."

Despite it being early morning, Petra-chan, who had come to pick her up, left with a radiant smile and led Beatrice toward school. The two of them are close friends who do everything together, but it seems they are also on the executive committee for the school trip, and they couldn't hide their sense of freshness at going to school at a different time than usual.

As a brother, I worry about my sister, who has high all-rounder stats but occasionally shows clumsiness, but I feel relieved knowing Petra-chan will fill in the gaps well. With those two as a unit, they could surely take over the world.

"It's a bit lonely for me, but it's also a brother's duty to see off his sister's big moment with a smile... If my favorite can make it to Budokan, I can die happy..."

"Whoa there, you're getting ahead of yourself, son. Our daughter is certainly a lovely girl who wouldn't be out of place center-stage at Budokan, but Papa won't allow that for at least another five years."

My father spoke to me as I stood at the entrance, seeing my sister off with a refreshing smile and slightly teary eyes. My father has a high rate of being half-naked, but on days when someone else's child like Petra-chan visits, he properly wears clothes. His T-shirt had Beatrice's smiling face printed on it; it was an item that allowed him to feel her loveliness by his side even on days when she went to school early.

"Don't worry. On the back, I've properly printed your smiling face from when you were little."

"I wasn't worried about that, and please be careful with the sense of distance from your teenage son. Beako aside, you never know what might trigger a family collapse with a boy this age."

"Don't say such scary things. It's true the smiling face on the back is from your elementary school days, but I love you just as much now that you're a high schooler. You know that, right?"

"I'm told that at least once a day."

It's a family rule in our house to say "I love you" once a day and to tell the people you like that you like them. I respect my father for valuing that, but as a teenage boy, it's a rather difficult rule to follow.

"But saying it to Beako or Mom isn't really embarrassing, so maybe it's just about daily conduct?"

"Hmph, how uncute! So, Beatrice left early, but what about you? You've got plenty of time; want to wrestle with me until Rem-chan gets here?"

"Waking up early just to wrestle with my father? Since when did I become a Hinomaru sumo boy? Still, just spacing out is also a waste... Alright."

It was annoying to follow my father's suggestion, but since I didn't want to spend time on something as fruitless as wrestling with him, I quickly put on my shoes at the entrance. A rare early morning, a rare early wake-up. Since I'm at it, I'll go for a morning stroll.

"They say the early bird catches the worm, so maybe something good will happen."

"I see, a walk. Wait a bit. I'll go change."

"I'm not going with you, Dad! I'm going alone! Go flirt with Mom!"

"Hm, I see. Sorry about that. It feels like you're being considerate."

"Leaving parents alone together isn't called being considerate! Stop acting like you're pushing the back of a classmate who's shy about romance!"

After that uneasy exchange, I dejectedly left the house. Somehow, it was a conversation that made it awkward to return immediately. My parents have been married for about twenty years, but they're a couple whose passion from their honeymoon days hasn't cooled, so they're difficult for their son and daughter to handle. Of course, I'm grateful that my parents get along so well.

"A happy home free of fights... Some say that clashing builds bonds, but nothing beats smooth sailing."

It's my theory that the fewer troubles, misfortunes, and obstacles, the better. So, while the Natsuki family is a very ordinary middle-class family, being blessed with good family relationships and a cute sister is more than enough—it's a hundred percent.

"Gratitude for the miracle of being born in this era, a thumbs-up with my entire existence, the sun shining in the sky lighting up the radiant future, don't block my path, I'm a runaway train."

Oh, my tension rose and I started rapping to myself. I see, waking up early really clears the head. Walking through the streets with slightly fewer people than usual while basking in the cool morning breeze is quite elegant—

"—Ah, good morning, Subaru. What a coincidence to see you here."

"Uhyee!?"

I was startled by the sudden voice just as I had started skipping. And for good reason; there was a possibility my previous rap had been heard, and on top of that, the voice I heard was like a beautiful silver bell. There was only one person in my history who shone brilliantly enough to match that voice.

"E-Emilia-tan? Why do you exist so early in the morning!?"

"Fufu. I know you're surprised, but 'exist' is a bit much. I'm properly carrying out biological activities even when Subaru isn't looking, you know."

"T-That's true. Haha, hehe, I was just surprised, fuhehe..."

Oh no, I laughed like a suspicious person because of the unexpected encounter. However, ignoring my deep reflection, the girl before me—the transfer student Emilia—turned her gentle eyes toward me and smiled like an angel.

She was a girl with long, beautiful silver hair like moonlight and slender limbs. Since she had just transferred, her uniform wasn't ready yet, so she wore her previous girls' academy uniform at school, but not here. A thin shirt and a skirt that flared softly—she was in casual clothes.

"Oh man, your casual clothes are cute..."

"Eh? Really? Thank you. Subaru, those clothes are... umm."

"Ah, this? This is my sweet lovely sister, Beatrice. Isn't she cute? This is her face when she just woke up and her hair was all messy..."

In front of a tilting Emilia, I bragged about the T-shirt I was wearing. The one I was wearing was different from the one my dad had; it was the type with Beatrice's face printed on both the front and back. I appreciate my father's feelings, but when it comes to doting on Beatrice, mixing in the impurities of my own existence is very bad.

"Wow, she's your sister. She's a veeery cute girl!"

"Ooh, you get it? Yes, she's a truly cute sister. She's been a bit rebellious lately, but she used to follow me everywhere saying 'Ni-cha, Ni-cha'... Not to brag, but I raised Beako."

"Eh!? What about your father and mother!?"

"They helped too, of course, but well, even combining the two of them, it's a 7:3 ratio in my favor."

"I see. Subaru, you're a veeery hard worker..."

It was my usual light talk, but thanks to Emilia accepting everything so easily, my tongue was really sliding. Anyway, my initial shock had faded, and I now had the leeway to savor the joy of suddenly encountering a beautiful transfer student.

"By the way, what were you doing, Emilia-tan? You're quite early."

"Me? I was taking a walk. See, I just moved here to transfer, so I don't know this area at all. I have to study for Puck's sake too."

"I see, you're diligent... wait, Puck?"

"Yes, Puck. He's the cat we keep at my house... look, he's over there."

Following where Emilia pointed, I saw a grey furball curled up on a brick wall a short distance away, swaying a long tail. Looking closely, I could see it was a single cat. When the cat noticed my gaze, it turned its round, beady eyes toward me and—

"Yo."

"Eh!?"

"W-What's wrong!? Suddenly making a voice like that..."

Beside me, who was shocked by the sudden event, Emilia was also surprised by my reaction. But I'm the one troubled by her acting like it's normal. Because just now, that cat—

"It talked, right? It said 'yo' to me and raised a paw..."

"Eh? That can't be right. Honestly, saying things like that... even if you try to tease me, I won't be fooled."

However, when I tried to confirm it, Emilia wouldn't listen at all. No, rather, her reaction was the normal one. If Emilia had said, "That's right, Puck talks. It's veeery rare, isn't it?" I wouldn't have known how to handle it.

"Well, it must have been a mishearing or an illusion. Phew, you startled me."

"Right, right. Let's go with that. Subaru wouldn't be happy if Lia was startled or looked at him like he was a weird kid, right?"

"You're talking to me so naturally, you!"

The cat—Puck—who had hopped off the wall, spoke to me while curling up at my feet. Overwhelmed by his blatant inhumanity, I picked Puck up. Puck's paw pad was pressed against my face. What a fluffy feeling.

"For your information, I don't have any special ability to talk to animals. In other words, the weird one isn't me, it's you."

"Well, if Subaru wants to claim that, I won't stop you, but Subaru will be the one looked at strangely. Here, look."

Told that, I looked to my side. Emilia had her dark blue eyes wide open, looking back and forth between me and Puck, who were glaring at each other up close.

"Somehow, it really looked like Subaru and Puck were talking. And Puck isn't acting up at all even though it's his first time being held by someone else, that's rare."

"Is that so? By the way, how did it look to you, Emilia-tan?"

"Eh? It looked like Puck was meowing cutely, and Subaru was saying something with a mean face..."

"My reputation is terrible! I mean, is this for real..."

Judging from Emilia's reaction, it seemed Puck's voice really couldn't be heard by anyone but me. Slipping through my hands as I was shaken, Puck landed gracefully on the ground. Then, while washing his face with his paws,

"Don't worry, it's not like some weird story is going to start because of this. I'm not going to turn you into a magical girl, or grant your wishes, or start talking about the entropy of the universe, so feel free to make a contract with me."

"I'm not making a contract! The word 'contract' is nowhere near 'peace of mind'!"

"Just a joke, just a joke."

Laughing, Puck hopped up into Emilia's arms. Emilia caught Puck's body and stroked his back.

"But I'm glad Puck and Subaru are getting along. I was a little worried."

"Worried? Why?"

"Puck doesn't usually get along with my friends... ah, is it okay if Subaru is my friend? Am I being too forward?"

"No, no, no, not at all! Not at all! In fact, I'm honored to be your first friend. Eventually, I'd even like to aim for a rank-up from the friend position."

"Sorry, I don't really understand what you're saying."

It's my bad habit to say unnecessary things when my tongue starts sliding. Anyway, meeting Emilia like this must be some kind of fate—

"Since you were out for a walk anyway, can I join you? We're both going to school eventually, so let's call this a date."

"A d-date... going out with a boy! It really is!"

"Oh, was it your first time by any chance?"

Seeing Emilia's eyes sparkle, I remembered she was raised in a girls' academy from elementary through high school. For a sheltered schoolgirl, she probably hadn't had many chances to go out with a boy. In that case—

"This is my first time d-dating... what do we do?"

"Wait! If it's Emilia-tan's first date, I want to do it properly! At least, this outfit with my sister's face printed on the front, back, and armpits is not appropriate!"

"Hidden Beatrice-chans in the armpits...!"

In the first place, I'm the type who wants to do things properly if I'm going to consider it a date. If it's a moment that will be carved into my memories, I want to set the stage.

"Thinking about it calmly, I don't have any dating experience either..."

"Is that so? But Subaru seems to get along with everyone in class..."

"No, if it's just simply going out with girls, I do it quite often. I go shopping with my childhood friend Rem all the time, and I'm dragged along for errands by Rem's sister, Ram."

But isn't it too sad to count those kinds of outings with childhood friends? Of course, I've helped with student council errands and gone shopping with Crusch, Ferris, and Anastasia, and I've been dragged around by a grumpy Priscilla when her attendant Al couldn't be found. I've also played with the six beautiful sisters next door—

"Thinking about it that way, I might look experienced from the outside..."

"Your appearance and contents don't match. Yeah, yeah, I get it. Subaru feels like that kind of guy."

"Shut up, you cat! Don't read people's minds!"

I yelled at Puck, who was purring in Emilia's arms. But Puck acted like he didn't hear me, and Emilia gave me a puzzled look. Damn it.

"Agh, anyway, today's walk is not a date. This is a walking date... yes, it's a separate count from a real date. Let's go with that."

"I don't really get it, but if that's okay with you, then fine. So, what should we do?"

"Hmm, let's see. Emilia-tan, you're basically trying to get a handle on the geography around your house while picking out a walking route for Puck, right?"

Thinking calmly, unlike dogs, I don't have the image of cats being walked by their owners, but since she didn't seem to have any doubts about it, I didn't point it out. Now, I was wondering what kind of course to introduce—

"As for me, I'd be happy with a place with a good view and a pleasant breeze. I don't want to exercise much, so avoid places with lots of slopes... Also, according to the Cat Treaty, I haven't greeted the stray cats in this area yet. So, I'd appreciate it if you avoided places that the boss cat has claimed as territory."

"You're giving me very specific orders... no, that's actually helpful."

"Right? It's troublesome when someone says 'anywhere is fine' on a date."

While scowling at Puck's knowing tone, it's easier to narrow down candidates if the selection criteria are strict. And so, the course I chose for my walking date with Emilia was—

"Alright, then let's go to the riverbank. There's a big river flowing right next to it, so it's scenic and feels great."

△▼△▼△▼△

And so, with that invitation, we arrived at our town's riverbank. This riverbank, with a famous large river flowing right beside it, is known as a prized spot for the first sunrise of the year, and on weekdays, it's known as a jogging course for fitness-minded youths and seniors.

Of course, there are many people visiting the riverbank for the same purpose as us, and you can see dog owners walking their pets and chatting happily here and there. You can see them, but—

"As expected, I don't see any cat owners..."

"Cats hate having collars and leads put on them, so maybe that's why... but Puck often comes to wake me up saying he wants to go for a walk."

"Heh, is that so?"

"Yep, yep. After all, it doesn't feel like a new day unless I'm with Lia."

Even if the purpose is walking a pet, the details differ, so Emilia looked troubled while holding Puck. Emilia's claim sounded like a pet owner just saying what they wanted, but according to Puck's actual thoughts, it wasn't unrequited. My prejudice that most cats in the world think of their owners as servants seemed to be wrong.

"By the way, even though it's a walk, Emilia-tan has been holding him the whole time."

"—? Yes, Puck doesn't like walking, so I always carry him. But occasionally, when he gets veeery chubby, I make sure he exercises properly."

"I see. Somehow, I had a preconception that once an animal's physique collapses, it can never go back... but you can do that kind of RIZAP thing."

"Well, it's the result of hard work."

"Getting fat was the result of sloth, though..."

I slumped my shoulders at Puck's triumphant face. Well, for now, he's thin, so he doesn't seem to have any intention of leaving Emilia's arms. As long as Emilia doesn't find him heavy, I have nothing to say.

"But isn't it hard to keep holding an adult cat? Puck is on the smaller side, though."

"It's okay. I might not look it, but I'm actually a bit of a strong person. Even at my old school, whenever there was heavy lifting, people would rely on me right away."

"Being called strong by the standards of girls' academy ladies doesn't really..."

Even if she appeals her strength with her thin, white arms, her upper arms look soft, so it's not convincing. For the record, I'm reasonably confident that I'm impressive when I take my clothes off. Most of these problems stem from not being able to see oneself objectively, so I suppose Emilia and I are on equal footing in that regard. And as we were talking like that—

"—Oh? Subaru-kun, what are you doing here?"

I was surprised to be called by a very familiar voice. I didn't even need to turn around to confirm who it was. After all, it was a voice more familiar to me than my own family's.

"Eh? Rem? What are you doing here?"

"I was accompanying Nee-sama this morning. But to think I'd meet Subaru-kun... as expected, Nee-sama's divine favor is outstanding."

"Even if you treat her like a shrine deity, Ram is just your sister."

I gave a wry smile to Rem, who was saying her usual nonsensical things. Standing on the riverbank in her usual way was Rem in cute casual clothes. She often comes over to play on days off, so there's no novelty, but adding the characteristic of a weekday morning suddenly made her look radiant. Hmm, the weekday effect.

"Hey, hey, Subaru. That girl is in our class, right?"

"Hm? Ah, yeah. This is Rem. My childhood friend who's all-around capable at housework."

I felt Emilia poking my back, and I reflected on leaving her out. Then, stepping to the side, I introduced Rem to Emilia. After that,

"Rem, this is Emilia-tan, as you know. We met by chance while she was walking her cat, so we're currently selecting a walking course suitable for a lazy cat."

"I see, so that's how it was. —Allow me to introduce myself again, I am Rem. Subaru-kun and I have been close since the time we shared our first bath."

"Don't lie. The one you shared your first bath with was Nee-sama."

"That's right. I meant we were wrapped in the same swaddling clothes."

"That was also Nee-sama."

Well, I don't know since I wasn't conscious right after birth, but since Rem and I have birthdays about two months apart, I expect that didn't happen.

"Ah, thank you for the polite introduction. I'm Emilia. This is the grey cat, Puck."

"A self-introduction like a delivery service..."

"Wow, what a lovely cat. May I touch him?"

"Of course. Here, Puck, show your belly."

"Can't be helped, nya."

While Puck looked quite pleased as he turned over, Emilia and Rem were giggling and playing with him. Watching them with my arms crossed, I tilted my head.

"Rem, you said you were accompanying Nee-sama, but why the riverbank? What does it have to do with Ram?"

"That's because Nee-sama is currently in special training."

"Special training?"

"Yes."

Rem smiled proudly while tickling Puck's belly with her finger. When Rem brags to someone like this, it's always about Ram or me. Setting Ram aside, being bragged about to others is embarrassing, but since I also brag about Rem when I talk to people, I guess we're even.

"Rem-san's sister is the Ram-san who was in our class, right?"

"Yes, that's right. She is a wonderful Nee-sama, far too good for Rem."

"And Rem's sister-bias is already at the level of a chronic illness, so take what she says with a grain of salt."

"Mou, that's not true."

Rem puffed out her cheeks and turned away. While still turned away, she began to talk about Ram, saying, "And so."

"As Subaru-kun knows, Nee-sama has outstanding athletic ability and is often asked to help out by various clubs... but..."

"Yeah, that's true. I often see her being active in various clubs. The way she handles herself even among boys is a natural talent..."

Ram's unusual trait is that she becomes good at whatever she tries immediately, especially in events that require sense; she's remarkably skilled at anything. So, in that regard, Rem's exaggerated praise isn't entirely wrong.

"So, what happened with the amazing Ram-san?"

"Yes. Since she's such a reliable Nee-sama, she was once again asked to help out a certain club... but she said she wanted to take it seriously, so she started morning training."

"Ram did!? Something so commendable!?"

It was quite a shock—honestly, it was news to me. Originally, Ram felt like someone who lived off her natural talent alone; she's basically haughty, overconfident, and has always produced results to match. So, I thought she was a stranger to anything resembling hard work.

"Besides, she's supposed to be obsessed with taking care of the novelist Roswaal-san right now, right? She even dragged you along to help, didn't she?"

"That's true, but she's working hard on this separately. So, Rem also wanted to support those feelings and made these preparations."

"Wow, amazing. So that's why it's like a picnic."

Behind the enthusiastic Rem, there were picnic baskets, water bottles, a first-aid kit for emergencies, and towel sets laid out on a leisure sheet. Always being perfectly prepared is Rem's virtue, but her flaw of overthinking and preparing unnecessary things was also showing.

"Well, Ram must be satisfied with all this... but for Ram to have to go this far, what kind of club did she agree to help?"

"The girls' judo club."

"Girls' judo club... did our school have one of those? I didn't know."

Even hearing Rem's story, the image of a girls' judo club didn't click for me. Our Lugunica Academy is a school where club activities are quite popular, so they often get decent results in prefectural tournaments and such. So, famous clubs usually reach your ears naturally.

"After all, our Handicrafts Club and Home Economics Club are regulars at the national tournament."

"Heh, is that so? That's amazing... wait! Nationals for Handicrafts and Home Economics!?"

"Oh, were you wondering what they do? For your information, it's quite athletic... it wouldn't be an exaggeration to call them cultural sports clubs. They compete in the speed of running stitches, how many needles they can thread within a time limit, and the precision of setting up a sewing machine..."

Handicrafts can be surprisingly deep when you push the competitive aspect. By the way, our school is a regular at the national tournament, but in most cases, the Handicrafts Club and Home Economics Club participate at the same time and end up crushing each other. We just accept it as a proxy war between the advisors, Frederica-sensei and Clind-sensei.

"Well, the most famous one at our school is the Light Music Club led by Liliana-chan. They even do national tours; they're quite something for an indie band."

"That doesn't sound like club activities at all anymore!"

"Lugunica Academy seems to have loose discipline in that regard, so it's allowed. It seems Liliana-sensei's passion reached Principal Wilhelm."

"The legendary direct appeal where Liliana-chan's teeth-guitar struck Principal Wilhelm's heart... I wish I could have seen it while I was still a student."

Liliana-chan and the Principal might seem like opposites, but since they both share a passion for the things they love, they actually get along quite well. Liliana-chan was apparently so moved by the Principal's stories of his wife that "Sword Demon Love Song" is a very popular song in the Light Music Club's setlist, and a photo of the Principal and his wife is even used for the jacket.

"Anyway, thinking about Liliana-chan is just idle talk, so setting that aside... I get the story about the girls' judo club, but why is Ram training?"

"That is..."

"—To want to know every single detail of Ram's actions, what a lewd man."

"Uhyowai!?"

I jumped at the sudden cold voice from behind. Interrupting Rem's answer and digging directly into my eardrums was my other childhood friend, Ram. Ram, in a hoodie and jogging shoes, was breathing slightly hard as she stared at me with her light crimson eyes.

"So, why is Barusu here? Surely you didn't hear that Ram was training and come all this way just to tease her?"

"I wouldn't do something so idle. As Nee-sama knows, I'm the type who wants to secure every last second of sleep. That's why I chose a school closest to my house."

"Because Barusu chose a school for such a reason, Ram had no choice but to go to that school too... haah. How selfish."

Ram sighed as she wiped a drop of sweat from her forehead with her finger. Behind the exchange between Ram and me, I heard Emilia softly whisper to Rem.

"Hey, why did Ram have to go to the school Subaru chose?"

"That's simple. Because Rem had decided to go to whatever school Subaru-kun went to. And since Rem wanted to attend the same school as Nee-sama, she asked Nee-sama to do so as well."

"I see. ...Wait, so it was Rem-san's selfishness?"

"Yes. Nee-sama is far too kind for listening to Rem's selfishness."

Rem blushed slightly, savoring the feeling of being loved by her sister.
